Game Modification: Dice with a Superstitious Spin
I added several rules to a regular game of rolling dice inspired by various superstitions to make it more exciting.

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Label Maker
Label Maker is a game where players bid on musical artist cards in an attempt to build the most lucrative record label. Artist cards can be combined to form supergroups, engines which generate increasing amounts of money for the label. Play ends when a certain score threshold is met.
PLAY
Hypertext Fiction: Adrift
'Adrift' is a work of hypertext fiction where you must survive a shipwreck in the midst of a great storm.
PLAY
Interactive Fiction:Letters
In 'Letters,' the player explores an empty house, piecing together what happened by locating and reading a series of letters.
